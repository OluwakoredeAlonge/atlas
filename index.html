About Atlas Motels & Resort

Since 1979, Atlas Motels & Resort has been Ekiti's cornerstone of exceptional hospitality. Founded by the late Otunba Olayiwola Igadan, our establishment has grown into a premier destination offering world-class accommodation, fine dining, and unforgettable event experiences.

Over four decades of excellence, serving generations with unwavering commitment to quality and service.
