Our Story

Founded in 1979 by the visionary late Otunba Olayiwola Igadan, Atlas Motel & Resort began as a modest retreat on Ikere Road in Ado-Ekiti. Over the decades, we've evolved into one of Ekiti State's most prestigious hospitality destinations, preserving our rich heritage while embracing modern excellence.

Today, under the continued stewardship of the Igadan family, Atlas Motel & Resort primes itself as a big player in the hospitality business in Ekiti State. We offer a serene atmosphere of privacy, security, and luxury for travelers who desire the best, featuring a large premium bar, affordable accommodation, and an excellent restaurant with a wide variety of delicacies.

1979 - The Foundation

Otunba Olayiwola Igadan establishes Atlas Motel & Resort on Ikere Road, introducing quality hospitality to Ado-Ekiti with a vision of excellence and genuine Nigerian warmth.

1990s - Culinary Excellence

Launch of our signature restaurant and premium bar, establishing Atlas as Ekiti's destination for fine dining with diverse local and continental cuisine.

2000s - Expansion Era

Introduction of Legacy Gardens event venue and expanded accommodation facilities, making Atlas the premier choice for celebrations and gatherings across Ekiti State.

2010s - Modernization

Comprehensive upgrades including 24-hour electricity, enhanced security systems, business centre, and modern room amenities to meet international hospitality standards.

Today - Ekiti's Pride

Recognized as one of Ekiti State's most prestigious hotels, serving thousands of satisfied guests annually while maintaining our commitment to excellence, affordability, and authentic Nigerian hospitality.
